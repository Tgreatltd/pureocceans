WHY SUPPORT US ?



Pure Ocean’s goal is to mobilize civil society to support ambitious and innovative projects for the protection of fragile marine ecosystems and biodiversity.



PURE OCEAN IS A UNIQUE AND NECESSARY PROJECT.





Protect fragile marine ecosystems

emergency


Boost innovation and technological

knowledge of ecosystems


Guarantee our common future by
participating in the collective effort




ABOUT US

Pure Ocean is an endowment fund, that aims to
raise funds to support innovative projects for the
preservation of fragile marine ecosystems and
biodiversity.

OUR SCIENTIFIC COMMITTEE

Pure Ocean’s Scientific Committee, composed of
world-reknowned experts in marine biodiversity,
defines the criteria of our annual Call for Projects
and guarantees the scientific validity of the projects
chosen for support.

OUR PROJECTS

Each year, Pure Ocean’s Scientific Committee will
define the criteria for the call for projects. After the
selection of projects by the committee, a biannual
follow-up and assessment will be made.
